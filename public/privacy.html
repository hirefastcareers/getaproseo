Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

GetAProSEO ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use getaproseo.com.

1. Who We Are

GetAProSEO is operated as a sole trader business based in the United Kingdom. Our contact email is getaproseo@gmail.com.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • Website URLs — the URL you enter to generate your SEO report
  • Business description — any optional context you provide about your business
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details
  • Email address — collected by Stripe at checkout for payment and receipts
  • IP address — collected automatically to prevent abuse of our free preview feature

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate your SEO report
  • Process your payment via Stripe
  • Prevent abuse of our free preview feature
  • Improve the quality of our service
  • Respond to support enquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Stripe Payments

All payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. When you make a purchase, you are subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy. We receive confirmation of payment and your email address from Stripe but do not store your card details.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the site to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Stripe may set its own cookies during the checkout process.

6. Data Retention

IP addresses used for rate limiting are stored temporarily in server memory and are not persisted to a database. We do not retain your website URL or report content after your session ends.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a UK resident, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (Information Commissioner's Office) at ico.org.uk

8. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services:

  • Anthropic — to power our AI report generation. URLs and business context are sent to Anthropic's API. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — to process payments. See Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — to host our website. See Vercel's Privacy Policy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Questions about your privacy?

Contact us at getaproseo@gmail.com and we'll respond within 5 working days.
